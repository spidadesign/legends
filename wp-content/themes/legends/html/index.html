Limousine Service

Arriving in style is just the beginning with Legends Limousine service. We deliver on-time transportation backed by the integrity of an industry leader. Our reputation as one of New York's premiere transport services has been earned one client at a time over 20 years of superior customer care in the tri-state area and beyond. We specialize in reliable transport throughout New York, New Jersey, Connecticut as well as other surrounding states. New Yorkers who demand nothing but the best at affordable rates have made Legends Limousine one of the most popular transport services for the area's major airports including JFK, LGA, Newark, Westchester, Teterboro and Islip. Aside from our cutting edge dispatch technology, clients enjoy the benefit of easy-to-use personal and corporate accounts that offer unparalleled convenience. Customer safety is our top priority which is why each of our over 100 vehicles and trained drivers are 100% licensed and insured. With such precious cargo, peace of mind is priceless.

Legends Limousine is proud to boast a sterling safety record and we are backed by more than 20 years of reliable service. Our team of professionals is comprised of courteous chauffeurs with a wealth of experience behind the wheel to ensure that your ride is both efficient and secure. While we specialize in airport transportation, Legends Limousine offers more than just door to door service, 24 hours a day. Our broad spectrum of available services is specifically designed to meet your changing needs throughout the year. From proms to weddings to business functions, Legends Limousine is your partner in quality transportation services. Countless local clients and out of town visitors alike take advantage of our affordable rates around New York City to add that extra touch to a special evening. Our fleet of cars can accommodate a variety of requests, from late model luxury vehicles to a broad spectrum of stretch limousines to suit your tastes. We invite you to contact us today to discover the difference quality makes in your travel experience.
